privacy Policy

Your personal information is used only for court booking and account management.

  • One account is permitted per residential unit.
  • Unit information, email and WhatsApp details are stored for booking management.
  • User information will not be shared outside authorized administration.
  • Booking records may be reviewed for operational purposes.
  • Residents are responsible for maintaining accurate account information.
  • The administration may suspend accounts that violate community rules.
