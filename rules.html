rules

  • Bookings open weekly as announced by management.
  • Each unit may have one active account.
  • Maximum booking duration is 2 hours on regular days.
  • Thursday, Friday and Saturday bookings are limited to 1 hour per unit.
  • Court operating hours are from 2:00 PM to 11:00 PM.
  • Residents must arrive on time.
  • Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions.
  • Respect other players and community rules.
  • Administration may modify or cancel bookings when required.
